About Word Checker

Word Checker is a versatile tool designed to enhance your understanding of words by providing detailed definitions, synonyms, and antonyms. Whether you're writing, studying, or exploring the richness of language, our tool offers a simple and effective way to expand your vocabulary and comprehension.

This project showcases the integration of modern web technologies, including:

  • HTML: For structuring the website.
  • CSS: For styling and layout design.
  • JavaScript: To create interactive features.
  • API: External data sources to fetch definitions, synonyms, and antonyms dynamically.

The Word Checker project is in progress, with future enhancements planned, including improved API integration and additional features like pronunciation guides and usage examples.
